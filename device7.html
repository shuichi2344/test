Studio Display

Product Description

Experience stunning visuals and immersive sound with the Studio Display - Apple's premium 27-inch monitor designed for professionals and enthusiasts. With its exceptional color accuracy, high-resolution display, and advanced audio system, the Studio Display is perfect for content creation, multimedia, and everyday computing tasks.

Key Features

  • Stunning 5K Retina Display: Enjoy crisp, lifelike visuals with the Studio Display's 27-inch Retina 5K display, featuring over 14.7 million pixels and P3 wide color gamut for vibrant, true-to-life colors.
  • Immersive Sound: Experience rich, detailed sound with the Studio Display's high-fidelity six-speaker sound system, featuring spatial audio technology and support for Dolby Atmos.
  • Ultra HD Camera: Stay connected with crystal-clear video calls using the Studio Display's built-in Ultra HD camera with advanced image signal processing for enhanced clarity and detail.
  • Seamless Integration: Connect your Mac, iPad, or iPhone effortlessly with the Studio Display's Thunderbolt 4 ports, allowing for high-speed data transfer and power delivery.

Specifications

  • Display: 27-inch Retina 5K display with True Tone and P3 wide color gamut
  • Resolution: 5120 x 2880 pixels
  • Audio: High-fidelity six-speaker sound system with support for Dolby Atmos
  • Camera: Ultra HD camera with advanced image signal processor
  • Ports: Three Thunderbolt 4 ports, One HDMI port, Gigabit Ethernet port, 3.5mm headphone jack
  • Operating System: macOS Monterey

Price

The Studio Display starts at RM6,999. Additional charges may apply for optional stand and nano-texture glass.
