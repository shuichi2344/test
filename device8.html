pro Display XDR

Product Description

Introducing the Pro Display XDR - Apple's professional-grade display designed for the most demanding workflows. With its exceptional brightness, contrast ratio, and color accuracy, the Pro Display XDR delivers unparalleled image quality for professionals in video editing, photography, and design.

Key Features

  • Retina 6K Display: Experience stunning clarity and detail with the Pro Display XDR's Retina 6K display, featuring over 20 million pixels and a P3 wide color gamut for lifelike colors and true-to-life images.
  • Extreme Dynamic Range: With its industry-leading brightness of up to 1600 nits and a contrast ratio of 1,000,000:1, the Pro Display XDR delivers exceptional contrast and detail in both highlights and shadows.
  • Precise Calibration: Each Pro Display XDR undergoes precise color calibration at the factory, ensuring consistent color accuracy and performance across multiple displays.
  • Compatibility: Connect your Mac Pro, MacBook Pro, or MacBook Air with Thunderbolt 3 to the Pro Display XDR for seamless integration and maximum productivity.

Specifications

  • Display: 32-inch Retina 6K display with True Tone and P3 wide color gamut
  • Resolution: 6016 x 3384 pixels
  • Brightness: Up to 1600 nits sustained, 1000 nits full-screen
  • Contrast Ratio: 1,000,000:1
  • Connectivity: Three Thunderbolt 3 ports, One USB-C port, One HDMI port
  • Operating System: macOS Monterey

Price

The Pro Display XDR starts at RM19,999. Additional charges may apply for optional stand and nano-texture glass.
