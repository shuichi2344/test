MacPro M2 Ultra Chip

Product Description

Meet the new Mac Pro with M2 Ultra chip - the ultimate powerhouse for professionals and enthusiasts. Designed for maximum performance and scalability, the Mac Pro redefines what's possible in a desktop workstation.

Key Features

  • M2 Ultra Chip: With the groundbreaking M2 Ultra chip, the Mac Pro delivers unparalleled performance, enabling you to tackle the most demanding tasks with ease.
  • Modular Design: Customize your Mac Pro with configurable options for processor, memory, storage, and graphics, allowing you to tailor it to your specific workflow requirements.
  • Advanced Cooling System: The Mac Pro features a sophisticated thermal architecture with three-dimensional airflow, ensuring optimal cooling performance under heavy workloads.
  • Expandability: With extensive expansion options, including PCIe slots, Thunderbolt ports, and USB-C ports, the Mac Pro provides unmatched flexibility for connecting peripherals and expansion cards.

Specifications

  • Processor: Apple M2 Ultra chip with up to 64-core CPU and 128-core GPU
  • Memory: Up to 1.5TB of DDR5 ECC memory
  • Storage: Up to 8TB SSD storage (Configurable to 16TB or 32TB)
  • Graphics: Up to four graphics cards with options including AMD Radeon Pro W6800X Duo, W6900X Duo, or NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3090
  • Expansion: Eight PCIe expansion slots, four Thunderbolt 4 ports, two USB-C ports, two 10Gb Ethernet ports
  • Operating System: macOS Monterey

Price

The Mac Pro with M2 Ultra chip starts at RM32,999. Pricing may vary depending on configuration options.
