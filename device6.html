Mac Studio M2 Max or M2 Ultra Chip

Product Description

Meet the new Mac Studio with M2 Max chip - the ultimate desktop workstation for professionals and creatives. With its powerful performance and customizable configuration options, the Mac Studio redefines what's possible in a desktop computer.

Key Features

  • M2 Max Chip: The M2 Max chip delivers industry-leading performance and power efficiency, allowing you to tackle the most demanding tasks with ease.
  • Customizable Configuration: Customize your Mac Studio with configurable options for processor, memory, storage, and graphics, ensuring it meets your specific workflow requirements.
  • Enhanced Audio: Experience immersive sound with the Mac Studio's studio-quality speakers and advanced audio processing capabilities, perfect for multimedia content creation and consumption.
  • High-Resolution Camera: Stay connected with crystal-clear video calls and recordings using the Mac Studio's built-in high-resolution camera, perfect for remote work and virtual meetings.

Specifications

  • Processor: Apple M2 Max chip with up to 40-core CPU and 128-core GPU
  • Memory: Up to 64GB of unified memory
  • Storage: Up to 8TB SSD storage (Configurable to 16TB)
  • Graphics: Up to four graphics cards with options including AMD Radeon Pro W6800X Duo, W6900X Duo, or NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3090
  • Expansion: Four Thunderbolt 4 ports, Two USB-A ports, Two 10Gb Ethernet ports
  • Operating System: macOS Monterey

Price

The Mac Studio with M2 Max chip starts at RM9,499, while the Mac Studio with M2 Ultra chip starts at $4,999. Prices may vary depending on configuration options.
