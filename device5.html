Mac Mini M2 or M2 Pro Chip

Product Description

Introducing the new Mac Mini with M2 chip - the compact powerhouse for everyday computing tasks. With its sleek design and powerful performance, the Mac Mini is perfect for home, office, or creative use.

Key Features

  • M2 Chip: The custom-designed M2 chip provides fast and efficient performance for all your computing needs, from web browsing to content creation.
  • Compact Design: With its compact and minimalist design, the Mac Mini fits seamlessly into any workspace, whether it's on your desk or tucked away in a corner.
  • Connectivity: Stay connected with a range of ports, including Thunderbolt 4, USB-C, HDMI, and Ethernet, allowing you to easily connect peripherals and external displays.
  • Built-in Speakers and Microphone: Enjoy clear audio for video calls and multimedia content with the Mac Mini's built-in speakers and microphone array.

Specifications

  • Processor: Apple M2 chip with up to 8-core CPU and 10-core GPU
  • Memory: 8GB unified memory (Configurable to 16GB)
  • Storage: 256GB SSD (Configurable to 512GB, 1TB, or 2TB SSD)
  • Ports: Two Thunderbolt 4 ports, Two USB-A ports, HDMI 2.0 port, Gigabit Ethernet, 3.5mm headphone jack
  • Operating System: macOS Monterey

Price

The Mac Mini with M2 chip starts at RM2,499, while the Mac Mini with M2 Pro chip starts at $999. Prices may vary depending on configuration options.
