MacBook Air 13" M1 Chip

Product Description

The MacBook Air 13" with M1 chip delivers powerful performance, stunning graphics, and incredible battery life. It features a sleek design, a brilliant Retina display, and a Magic Keyboard for a comfortable typing experience.

This MacBook Air is perfect for everyday tasks such as browsing the web, sending emails, editing documents, and more. With its energy-efficient M1 chip, you can enjoy up to 15 hours of battery life on a single charge, allowing you to work and play all day long.

Key Features

  • Lightning-fast Performance:The Apple-designed M1 chip delivers blazing-fast performance for seamless multitasking and smooth computing experience.
  • Brilliant Retina Display: Immerse yourself in vivid colors and sharp details with the stunning 13.3-inch Retina display featuring True Tone technology.
  • Touch ID: Securely unlock your MacBook Air and authenticate your identity with the Touch ID sensor for seamless login and secure transactions.
  • All-day Battery Life: Enjoy up to 15 hours of battery life on a single charge, so you can work, create, and play all day long without interruptions.

Specifications

  • Processor: Apple M1 chip with 8-core CPU and 7-core GPU
  • Memory: 8GB unified memory
  • Storage: 256GB SSD (Configurable to 512GB, 1TB, or 2TB SSD)
  • Display: 13.3-inch Retina display with True Tone
  • Battery Life: Up to 15 hours of web browsing
  • Operating System: macOS Big Sur
  • Ports: Two Thunderbolt/USB 4 ports, 3.5mm headphone jack
